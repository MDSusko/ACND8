ACND8 Music

From house to trance, and everything in between, ACND8 has spun across many genres of EDM and continues to uplift and inspire through his sets. His alt alias Wulfe, pushes the darker side of EDM with Techno and tech House. Spinning iconic locations such as Avalon Hollywood, Exchange LA, Catch One and Garage Gallery, in Los Angeles, and Bar Standard/Milk Bar, 1134, and Club Vinyl here in Denver.

ACND8 is actively contributing his Chicago roots and LA inspired vibe to bring a revitalized sound to the Denver EDM scene.

Throughout his career, ACND8, has continued to push the boundaries with his mixes, creating a unique journey across genres in his sets.

Going into 2024, ACND8, plans on releasing his first track, continuing to build his skills as a DJ, and teaching new DJ's the skills needed to succeed. 2024 also marks a decade of being a DJ for ACND8! For booking inquires please email: plurfxla@gmail.com
